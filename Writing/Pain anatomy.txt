Dorsal (back) side of spine is where dorsal root ganglions merge signals from the body and send them into the spine at the dorsal horn, where they go onto second order neuron.

Two types of pain sensing fibre (afferent fibres).
A-Fibres - fast, localised, myelinated. Sharp pain.
C-Fibres - slow, poorly-localised, un-myelinated. Burning, throbbing pain.
